Citation and Information
The gene search has autocomplete for genes writen in the version 5 of its nomenclature.
Still, you can also search any gene by synonyms, e.g:
-MtrunA17_Chr4g0035464
-Medtr4g070970
-Mt0003_00864
-Medtr_v1_024796
Any of those synonyms will search the same gene.
The search may take a bit long as the platform needs to connect to all web services to gather the gene data.
